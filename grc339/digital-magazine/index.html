Rattlesnake Ledge

Hiking Rattlesnake Ledge offers a moderately challenging trail that rewards hikers with stunning views of Rattlesnake Lake and the surrounding mountains. The trail is popular for its scenic beauty, especially at sunrise or sunset, making it a favorite among both locals and visitors.

Story about Tolmie Peak Trail

Tolmie Peak provides a moderately strenuous trek through dense forests and along beautiful alpine meadows.

Snoqualmie Falls

Snoqualmie Falls is a spectacular 268-foot waterfall, easily accessible to visitors year-round, known for its powerful cascades and misty spray.

Poo Poo Point

Poo Poo Point is a popular hiking destination that offers a moderately challenging ascent with rewarding views of the Issaquah Valley and Lake Sammamish.

Suncadia

Suncadia Trails offer a network of scenic pathways through the picturesque Suncadia Resort area, featuring a variety of trails suitable for hiking, biking, and wildlife viewing.
