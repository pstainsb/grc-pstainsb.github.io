Welcome to Mattone, where passion for Italian cuisine meets warm hospitality in a charming and inviting atmosphere. Our restaurant is more than just a place to dine; it's a gathering spot where friends and family come together to share laughter, stories, and of course, delicious food. Nestled in the heart of Seattle, Mattone exudes the timeless charm of an authentic Italian trattoria. From the moment you step through our doors, you'll be enveloped in the aromas of simmering sauces, freshly baked bread, and aromatic herbs, transporting you to the bustling streets of Italy.

At Mattone, we're committed to creating an experience that goes beyond just a meal. Our welcoming ambiance, attentive service, and dedication to quality ensure that every visit is memorable and every guest feels like part of our extended family. We believe in honoring tradition while embracing creativity in the kitchen. Our menu features a delightful selection of classic Italian dishes crafted with the finest ingredients, sourced locally whenever possible, and prepared with love and care by our talented chefs.

Mattone is more than just a restaurant; it's a labor of love that began generations ago in the small village of Capracotta, nestled in the picturesque countryside of Italy. The story of Mattone is rooted in the traditions and culinary heritage passed down through the generations of the Rossi family. Today, Mattone continues to thrive as a family-owned and operated establishment, with Nonna Maria's recipes still serving as the cornerstone of the menu. From the homemade pasta to the decadent desserts, every dish at Mattone is prepared with the same love and dedication that has been passed down through generations of the Rossi family. As you step through the doors of Mattone, you're not just dining at a restaurant – you're joining the Rossi family table, where every meal is a celebration of food, family, and the rich tapestry of Italian culture.
